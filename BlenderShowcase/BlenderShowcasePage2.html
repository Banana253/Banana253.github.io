Esports Mario Kart Tournament AD

Jan 31, 2023

This was created as a advertisement for a Mario Kart 8 Deluxe Tournament we held as a final in Esports Marketing. This class was tons of fun, and was the whole tournament was fun to put together, my Brother ended up winning the tournament that I helped host so naturally the joke that it was rigged started. This render was used a few times mainly on the class Instagram page, for reasons I've edited the texture on the trophy back to the normal on but when it was being used it had the school logo instead of the triforce. Also as part of the tournament I 3d printed this trophy model (with a good amount of modification) as a first place prize, it was safe to say that I had the most time on the class 3d printers that year. There are a few more renders that I made for that class that will come up later.
