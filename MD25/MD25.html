Here is a page to show the project I did for Mother's Day.



First, the full video:




I started this about a month ago when I got the main idea for this video, redoing the "Cannon Bit" but making it like a tv show.



Behind the Scenes, Way to Much:

I made a pretty big chunk of stuff that ends up not being seen, I hadn't finished the script when making the surroundings so I didn't know how much I would need. I ended up making our whole street, but every house is just a recolor of our house, kinda like real life.




Behind the Scenes, 2D:

Some things I used are just 2d Images or Videos.


This is just a video without a background.


Here is the raw video. Well, I mean its a screenshot of a video. But to make it I recorded myself in a Vr Game with leg tracking so I could capture my whole body in a avatar.


All the trees in the background are just pre rendered images, as well as a front and back of the house.



Behind the Scenes, Camera Blow Up:

To make the camera fly away, I found a model of a camera to get the physics to some what align with real life. Then had a ball grow really fast and hit it, the ball also makes the cannons fall over in the first place.



As a result the cannons just fly off, though it doesn't matter because the camera is going crazy.



The wind sound while the camera is flying, this sound is just my fan pointed at my mic. You can still kinda hear the fan in the background but it worked well. Very kind on the ears.
